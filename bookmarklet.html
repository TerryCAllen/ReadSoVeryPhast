Troubleshooting

Bookmarklet doesn't appear in toolbar

Make sure your bookmarks bar is visible. If dragging doesn't work, try right-clicking the button and selecting "Bookmark This Link" or "Add to Bookmarks."

No content extracted

Some pages (like PDFs or heavily JavaScript-dependent sites) may not extract well. The bookmarklet will fall back to extracting all visible text if Readability fails.

Page opens but no text appears

Check your browser's popup blocker settings. The bookmarklet needs to open a new tab with the SpeedRead app.

"Content too large for URL" error

Very rarely, extremely large documents may exceed browser URL limits (typically ~2MB). If this happens, try saving the content to a text file and pasting it directly into the app instead.
